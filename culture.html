Embracing My Nepali Heritage

Growing up in the vibrant landscapes of Nepal, I have been deeply influenced by the rich traditions, diverse communities, and the spirit of resilience that characterizes my country.

These principles guide my interactions in both personal and professional settings, fostering collaboration and understanding.
